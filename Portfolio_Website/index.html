About Me

Hello there, I'm Brandon Hawley, a passionate software architect, game enthusiast, and code virtuoso on an endless quest to turn imagination into reality.

With a keyboard as my trusty sword and lines of code as my spells, I embark on epic adventures in the world of code. My journey began with Java, and my skill tree only expanded from there. The elusive realms of C++, C#, Python, and more opened their doors to my quest. These languages, like languages of ancients, empower me to craft experiences that captivate and amaze in a new era.

When I'm not busy summoning code spells, you can find me exploring the uncharted territories of AI and machine learning, determined to unlock their potential in gaming and beyond.

The Quest...

My love for both the art and science of programming led me to dance with Unreal Engine and wield the mighty Unity Engine. In these domains, I shape worlds, breathe life into characters, and unleash the power of interactive storytelling.

But it's not just about the pixels and polygons; it's about the magic that happens when creativity meets code. The symphony of lines of text coming together to create awe-inspiring visuals and immersive gameplay is my true passion. Each project I embark upon is a canvas, where logic, innovation, and creativity unite to craft experiences that linger in the minds of players.

My ultimate dream? To take my place among the pioneers of game design, to contribute to narratives that resonate, and to inspire the next generation of digital storytellers.
