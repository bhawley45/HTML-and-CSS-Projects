Tier 1: CosmoNest Apartments

Elevate your Mars experience with our stackable apartment-style habitats. Practical, compact, and ingeniously designed, CosmoNest Apartments offer a cozy haven while optimizing space. These versatile habitats provide all the essentials you need to embark on your journey to the Red Planet.

Tier 2: TerraTower Residences

Experience Martian life in style within our cylindrical 3-story TerraTower Residences. Thoughtful layouts make the most of the limited footprint while ensuring comfortable living. These residences offer an ideal balance between efficient use of space and modern furnishings, allowing you to immerse yourself in the wonders of Mars.

Tier 3: Celestial Dodeca Villas

For those seeking unparalleled luxury and panoramic views, our Celestial Dodeca Villas redefine interplanetary living. The dodecahedron-shaped habitats boast expansive windows that frame breathtaking vistas of the Martian landscape. With lavish furnishings and ample space, these villas are a testament to both opulence and sustainability.
